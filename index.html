Hi, my name is Tobias!

I'm a modern .NET developer with a wide skill-set, a curiosity for technical solutions and strong passion for creativity.

I have experience with a wide array of tools and frameworks ranging from full-stack .NET development, database management and web-dev to 3d modelling applications, vector graphics and illustration software.

At present I am studying .NET development at IT-Högskolan, Gothenburg, and will be graduating in 2023. If you have any open positions or rooms for an intern then feel free to throw me an e-mail about it.

Over the years I have developed a mind for problem-solving and have recently found a love for programming at large.

Apart from keeping up with news, in tech as well as in general, I tend to spend my time practicing drawing, plucking away at my guitar or going for the occational painting session 'en plein air'. I've been creating in one form or another for as long as I can remember and I don't see myself stopping any time soon.
